Mission

Accelerate life sciences research with AI

Approach

We use LLMs to synthesize the literature, generate hypotheses, and see patterns at a scale no single researcher could.

Current Focus: Overview of Reviews

We are conducting an overview of reviews (umbrella review) to map existing evidence on automated certainty grading. Our goal is to identify durable methodological lessons and avoid redundant research.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Protocol & Planning (Current) – Defining usage of PRIOR guidelines and AMSTAR 2 appraisal.
  • Phase 2: Execution – Searching, screening, and appraising existing reviews.
  • Phase 3: Synthesis & Reporting – Mapping evidence gaps and separating durable lessons from hype.
